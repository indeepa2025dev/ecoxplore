River Rafting

Rainforest River Raft

⏱ 4 Days / 3 Nights 👥 Max 8 guests 🌟 4.8/5 (74 reviews) ● Moderate

Navigate Grade III rapids as the river cuts through one of the world's oldest rainforests. Between rapids, you'll drift past towering ferns, spot river otters and kingfishers, and camp on secluded white-sand banks accessible only by water. Our rafts are inflatable, repair-kit equipped, and 100% PVC-free.

What's Included

  • Certified swift-water rescue guide
  • Raft, paddles, dry bags, wetsuit & helmet
  • 3 nights' riverside eco-camping
  • All meals cooked over eco-camp stoves
  • Rainforest ecology guide booklet
  • Carbon offset certificate
$749 / person
