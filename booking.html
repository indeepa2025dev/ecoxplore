Common Questions

Before You Book

What is included in the tour price? +

All tour prices include a certified guide, all meals from the first dinner to the final breakfast, all specialist equipment, accommodation (eco-camp or eco-lodge depending on tour), and a carbon offset certificate. Flights, travel insurance, and personal spending money are not included.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We offer free cancellation up to 30 days before your departure date. Cancellations between 15–30 days prior incur a 25% fee. Within 14 days, the full tour cost is non-refundable. We strongly recommend travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations.

Are these tours suitable for beginners? +

Yes — many of our tours are designed for first-timers. The Highland Wilderness Trail, Coastal Cycling Loop, and Dark Sky Wilderness Camp are all suitable for beginners. Each tour listing clearly displays the difficulty level. If you're unsure, just ask us in your enquiry.

Can I join as a solo traveller? +

Absolutely! Solo travellers are welcome on all tours. You'll be joining a small group of like-minded adventurers. Solo booking doesn't incur a single supplement on most tours — please enquire for details.
