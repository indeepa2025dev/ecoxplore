Our Story

From a single hiking trail to a global eco-adventure brand — built on passion, purpose, and respect for the natural world.

🌲
Our Mission

Adventure That Gives Back

EcoXplore Adventures was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who believed that the best adventures leave a place better than they found it. What started as weekend hiking trips in the highlands has grown into a globally recognised eco-tourism company offering a full range of low-impact outdoor experiences.

We partner with local communities, conservation organisations, and indigenous guides to create tours that are authentic, educational, and environmentally responsible. Every booking contributes to our reforestation fund — to date, we've planted over 18,000 trees across 6 countries.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make — from trip design to supplier selection.

🌍

Environmental Stewardship

We leave no trace. All waste is packed out, routes are carefully managed, and we work with park authorities to protect fragile ecosystems.

🤝

Community First

We hire local guides, source food locally, and ensure a fair share of revenue stays within host communities.

🔬

Education & Awareness

Every tour includes interpretation sessions so guests leave with a deeper understanding of the ecosystems they've explored.

🛡️

Safety & Responsibility

Comprehensive risk assessments, fully trained guides, and 24/7 emergency support keep every guest safe at all times.

♿

Inclusivity

We believe adventure should be accessible. We offer adapted tours and actively welcome guests of all abilities.

💡

Transparency

We publish our annual environmental and financial impact reports so guests can see exactly where their money goes.
